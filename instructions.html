Star Warriors Guide

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Move Ship
  • Spacebar: Shoot
  • Left click: Rocket/Bomb
  • B: Decoys
  • Escape: Pause / Resume Game

Gameplay:

  • Survive waves of enemy ships.
  • Defeat the final boss.
  • Choose different ships and modes for varied gameplay.

Modes:

  • Normal: Standard health (3) and ammo.
  • Cheat: Invincible with unlimited ammo and super rockets.
  • Derek: Increased HP and damage, one-hit kills (except the boss).

Ships:

  • Light Interceptor: Fast, low health, no shields. Good fit if you are strong with the Force.
  • Interceptor: Balanced speed, health, and firepower, high shields. Good fit for an average human.
  • Bomber: Slow, high health, very high shields, powerful bombs with 2 mods:
    • Attack the closest enemy with 2 bombs.
    • Attack two closest enemies with 1 bomb each.

Decoys:

  • Serve as bait for rockets and bombs.
  • Click 'B'/'b' to launch a decoy.
  • Cooldown: 1 sec, refresh: 3 sec.

Rockets/Bombs:

  • Rockets are fast with auto aim, damage: 3.
  • Bombs are slow, also with auto aim. Both bombs and rockets last for 10 seconds.

Astromechanic

  • Fixes the ship per fixed time of 3 seconds
